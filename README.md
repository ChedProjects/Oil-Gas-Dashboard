# Oil & Gas Production & Revenue Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)
Author: Cheddy Emile Tools Used: Excel, Power Query, Microsoft Power BI, DAX

Datasets OGORBcsv.csv — Raw Monthly U.S. Federal Oil & Gas Production & Disposition Data (2015–2025) real-oil-price.csv — Historical U.S. WTI Oil Pricing Data (1986–2026) Personal-Project.csv — Combined and transformed dataset used for dashboard modeling and analysis Overview

This project analyzes U.S. oil & gas production trends, commodity pricing, and estimated revenue performance using publicly available operational datasets and historical WTI crude oil pricing data.

Oil production data was sourced from publicly available datasets, while oil pricing data was obtained from the U.S. Energy Information Administration.

Excel and Power Query were used for data cleaning, transformation, and integration, while Power BI was used to develop an executive-level business intelligence dashboard.

The dashboard was designed to support financial, operational, and strategic decision-making within the energy sector through KPI reporting, geographic analysis, time-series modeling, and revenue trend analysis.

State-level weighted average WTI pricing was used to model estimated revenue performance across production regions.
